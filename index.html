Tips & Tricks for Successful Farming

1. Soil Preparation

Test your soil before planting. Add compost or organic fertilizer to improve soil health and water retention.

2. Crop Rotation

Rotate your crops each season to prevent soil depletion and reduce pest buildup.

3. Water Management

Use drip irrigation or water early in the morning to minimize water loss and fungal diseases.

4. Organic Pest Control

Use natural pest repellents like neem oil, or introduce beneficial insects such as ladybugs.
